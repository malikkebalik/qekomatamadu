Ravens VS 49ers Super Bowl 2024: Leaked Script By News Station

Publish date: 2024-08-07

In a surprising twist, the script for the Ravens VS 49ers Super Bowl 2024 was allegedly leaked, creating mass hysteria and conspiracy theories.

The NFL playoffs 2024 are in full flow, with the conference championship rounds happening next weekend.

However, a Canadian public TV network has ignited enthusiasm among NFL conspiracy theorists by suggesting that the Super Bowl might be rigged.

Super Bowl 2024 will be played at Allegiant Stadium in Las Vegas, Nevada, on February 11, 2024.

It will be one of the most exciting days of the sporting calendar, with millions of fans waiting in anticipation.

With the conference championship games next week, only four teams are remaining.

However, in a funny gaffe, a Canadian broadcast network might have leaked the script for the Super Bowl.

Ravens VS 49ers Super Bowl 2024: Script Leaked?

A picture from the January 18 CTV News Vancouver Island broadcast is circulating online. This is because of an intriguing depiction of the Super Bowl scheduled for February 11.

In the flex placed in the bottom right corner, they showed the Super Bowl match between the Baltimore Ravens and the San Francisco 49ers.

“Reba McEntire, Usher, and Post Malone are slated to perform at the San Francisco 49ers & Baltimore Ravens Super Bowl match-up on February 11.”

Interestingly, the broadcast was released before the start of the second round of the playoffs.

This prompted numerous social media users to speculate (many jokingly) that the NFL’s script for the playoffs was leaked.

Some fans claimed the moment to be a troll, while others said the NFL will have some explaining to do if the prediction comes true.

Naturally, this has excited conspiracy theorists among NFL fans, who claim the league is rigged.

Subsequently, the Ravens and the 49ers both went on to win their divisional-round playoff fixtures days later. This has added even more fuel to the speculations.

All that remains now for the CTV prophecy to come true is the Ravens and 49ers winning their conference playoffs.

Regardless of the jokes, this is most likely a mistake on the part of the broadcasting station.

In fairness to them, the Ravens and 49ers are the odds-on favorite to make the big game.

Undoubtedly, this is an exciting period for football fans worldwide.

With the Ravens facing the Chiefs and the 49ers up against the Lions, fans wait with bated breath to see who makes it to the Super Bowl.

Overall, a graphical gaffe from CTV has led to a light-hearted exchange between football fans joking that the NFL is rigged.

More On Super Bowl 2024

Apart from the athletes and teams, the Super Bowl is also exciting for fans of the glamour world.

With the Halftime Show one of the most watched events on television, there is always intrigue about who will perform.

We can report that singer and songwriter Usher will be headlining the Super Bowl LVIII Halftime Show.

After the announcement in January, Usher described the opportunity as a lifelong goal.

“It’s an honor of a lifetime to finally check a Super Bowl performance off my bucket list. I can’t wait to bring the world a show unlike anything else they’ve seen from me before.”
