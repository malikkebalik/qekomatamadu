Odell Beckham Jr. rocks Travis Hunter shirt after Colorado win

Publish date: 2024-09-17

Odell Beckham Jr. is on the Colorado wave, too.

About 12 hours after Colorado’s come-from-behind, double-overtime win against rival Colorado State, Beckham wore a T-shirt featuring Buffaloes star Travis Hunter during warmups ahead of the Ravens’ Week 2 game against the Bengals.

The Baltimore receiver also sported cleats inspired by legendary NFL wideout Randy Moss.

Hunter, a two-way star, dominated in his opening week performance for the Buffaloes when he recorded 11 catches for 119 yards on offense and added an interception against TCU, who fell to Georgia in January’s national championship game.

Hunter famously played 129 snaps in the stunning 45-42 upset.

The 6-foot-1 receiver and defensive back was part of the “luggage” head coach Deion Sanders brought from Jackson State to Colorado upon taking the job prior to this season.

He recorded two catches for 21 yards during the team’s dramatic 43-35 overtime win Saturday night, but was leveled by a big hit that Sanders says will sideline him for “a few weeks.”

Beckham, who signed a one-year deal, $15 million deal with the Ravens this past offseason, recorded two catches for 37 yards in his debut with the team during Week 1 against the Houston Texans.

The Ravens won the game, 25-9.
