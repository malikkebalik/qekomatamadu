A Glimpse of Mastroianni's Professional Life

Hey Bartender actress, Mia Mastroianni started working as a bartender professionally after moving to Los Angeles, California. At that time, she discovered her passion for the craft cocktail movement. Later, she began exploring bar tools, techniques, ingredients to make cocktails.

Mia then joined Spike TV's reality show, Bar Rescue as one of Jon Taffer's bartending and mixology experts in 2012. During the show, she was excellent in giving out recipes for cocktails, margaritas, and many other beverages. Her famous cocktail recipe was of Strawberry-Sweet tea cocktail.

Mastroianni made her appearance in reality TV shows, including Catch 21, FabLife, Morning Buzz Live with Nick Lachey. Recently, she guests appeared in Ray DuGray and Maria Ngo's talk-show, THE STRIP LIVE in 2021.
