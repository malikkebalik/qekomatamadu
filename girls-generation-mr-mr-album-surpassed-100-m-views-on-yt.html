Girls' Generation Mr. Mr. Album Surpassed 100 M Views On YT

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Girls’ Generation, or SNSD for short, is a female group from South Korea that was created by SM Entertainment. Taeyeon, Sunny, Tiffany, Hyoyeon, Yuri, Sooyoung, Yoona, and Seohyun are the eight members of the group. When Jessica left the group in September 2014, it was down to eight members. Girls’ Generation is one of the most celebrated celebrities in South Korea and a global phenomenon, earning them the title of “The Nation’s Girl Group” and a slew of other awards.

Girls’ Generation Mr. Mr. Album

Girls’ Generation’s latest music video has surpassed 100 million views on YouTube. After “Gee,” “The Boys,” “Mr. Taxi,” “I Got A Boy,” “Oh!,” “Party,” “Run Devil Run,” and “Lion Heart,” the music video for Girls’ Generation’s 2014 smash “Mr. Mr.” exceeded 100 million views on YouTube on November 12 at roughly 3 p.m. KST. To put that in perspective, Girls’ Generation’s “Mr. Mr.” was first released on February 28, 2014 at 10:30 p.m. KST, so it has been out for a total of 9 years, 8 months, and 14 days.

Where did “Forever 1” Reach on the Circle Albums Chart?

For their 15th anniversary, SM Entertainment announced in May 2022 that Girls’ Generation would make a full-group comeback after a five-year absence. As part of their anniversary celebration, the band appeared on JTBC’s Soshi TamTam, a reality show that ran for eight episodes beginning in July. Forever 1, their seventh studio album in Korean, was released in August 2022, and it reached #2 on the Circle Albums Chart. In the same month as their sold-out “Long Lasting Love” special event in September 2022, the group also performed at the SM Town Live 2022 concert. After her exclusive contract with SM Entertainment concluded in August 2023, Sunny left the label.
