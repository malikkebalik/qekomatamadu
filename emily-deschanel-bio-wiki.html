Who is Emily Deschanel?

Emily Deschanel is an American actress and a producer. She has made and worked on many films. Her best role was for starring in the Fox crime procedural comedy-drama series Bones as Dr. Temperance Brennan from 2005 to 2017.

Age, Parents, Siblings, Family, Ethnicity, Nationality

She is the first daughter of Caleb Deschanel(father) and actress Mary Jo Deschanel(mother). She has a sister, Zooey Deschanel. Her nationality is American and her ethnicity is Mixed (Swiss, Dutch, English, Irish, and French).
