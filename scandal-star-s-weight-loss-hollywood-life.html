Kerry Washington Flaunts Sexy Post-Baby Body 5 Months After Giving Birth

It’s official: Olivia Pope has finally ditched the awkwardly oversized coats!

Just five months after giving birth to a beautiful baby girl named Isabelle, first-time mom Kerry has put her post-baby weight loss on display in a number of chic ensembles.

In the middle of heavy promotion of Scandal’s new season, Kerry stepped out on Sept. 22 and 23 in New York City to show off her trim waistline and sexy toned legs.

In a yellow dress, Kerry opted for a sexy plunging neckline, a form-fitting waist, and a beautiful flowing skirt that ended just below the knees.

The following day, Kerry slipped into a little blue number that really accentuated her figure and showed a little more leg with a hemline stopping just above the knee. Gorgeous!

‘Scandal’ Season 4 Preview Reveals Major Spoilers

Everyone wants to know: where are Olivia and Jake headed at the end of season three?

Well, the answer has finally been revealed in a new preview clip for the upcoming fourth season — a beach!

In the clip, Olivia is soaking up the sun on the beach as she relaxes on a luxurious lounge chair while sipping a glass of red wine. Even better? As Olivia is enjoying a little R&R, President Fitz is desperately calling her — and getting no answer. Uh oh!

Although this reveals Olivia’s location, that’s about all it reveals — so there are still a LOT of questions remaining for the season four premiere on Thursday, Sept. 25.
