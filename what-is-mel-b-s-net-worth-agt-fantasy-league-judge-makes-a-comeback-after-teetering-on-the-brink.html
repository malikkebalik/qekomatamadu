Mel B or Melanie Brown was born on M

ay 29, 1975, in Leeds, England. Before entering the media world, she studied performing arts at Intake High School in Leeds. She started her career as a dancer and later turned towards a singing career and joined a band called Touch.

After that, she joined the 'Spice Girls' which included Victoria Beckham, Emma Bunton, Geri Halliwell, and Melanie Chisholm. They made their breakthrough with their debut single 'Wannabe' in 1996 which was a hit in over 30 countries. 

'America's Got Talent: Fantasy League' judge Mel B's musical and television career

Mel B became a part of the Spice Girls in 1994 and sold more than 55 million albums worldwide. 'Hot' was her first solo album released on October 9, 2000, which entered the UK charts. 

After releasing a few singles, she parted with Virginia Records and started a successful career as a TV presenter and actress. In 2001, she had her show 'This is my moment', which was a great success. She then made a second documentary series about African voodoos.

In 2002, she was one of the protagonists of the BBC sitcom 'Burn It'. She also did a few films by then such as 'LD 50 Lethal Dose' (2003) and 'The Seat Filler' in 2004. 

Mel B is known worldwide as the 'Scary Spice' of the 'Spice Girls' group. As a part of this band, Mel B became a household name. 

Mel B shinned both as an actress and television personality. She is also the author of her best-selling autobiography 'Catch A Fire' which was released in 2002. This book contains her insights and experiences, both as a 'Spice Girls' member and her own life. 

Mel B is currently one of the four judges of NBC's 'America's Got Talent'. She also held judging spots on 'The X Factor' UK and Australia.

How much money does 'America's Got Talent: Fantasy League' judge Mel B make?

Mel B's financial struggles and how she overcame after a messy divorce

Even though Mel B was a millionaire at the time, her divorce and bad business investment led to her losing all of it.

Mel B faced IRS trouble of around 2.5 million in 2018. Mel B's financial success story is proof that no matter how hard you fail, you can succeed.
