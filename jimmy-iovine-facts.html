Jimmy Iovine’s Biography and Age

Born James Iovine on March 11, 1953, in Brooklyn, New York, Jimmy Iovine grew up in a working-class family. He attended John Jay College before dropping out to pursue his music career. In the early 1970s, Iovine began working as a recording engineer and producer, eventually becoming one of the most sought-after music producers in the industry.

As of 2021, Jimmy Iovine is 68 years old and is showing no signs of slowing down. He continues to be a force in the music business and has made a significant impact on the industry.

Jimmy Iovine’s Relationships and Children

Jimmy Iovine has been married twice. His first marriage was to Vicki McCarty, with whom he has two children, Jessica and Jamie. Iovine’s second marriage was to model Liberty Ross, whom he married in 2016. The couple welcomed their first child, a son named Balthazar, in 2018.

Jimmy Iovine has been in several high-profile relationships throughout his career. He famously dated Stevie Nicks of Fleetwood Mac and was engaged to music executive Denise Di Novi before marrying Vicki McCarty.

Jimmy Iovine’s Height

Jimmy Iovine is often described as short, and his height has been the subject of much speculation. While his exact height is not known, he is estimated to be around 5’4″.

Jimmy Iovine’s Career

Jimmy Iovine’s early career in music began as a sound engineer and producer for artists such as John Lennon, Bruce Springsteen, and Tom Petty. In the 1980s, he co-founded Interscope Records with music executive Ted Field. The label quickly gained a reputation for signing cutting-edge and controversial artists, including Dr. Dre, Tupac Shakur, and Marilyn Manson.

In 2006, Iovine partnered with Dr. Dre to create the Beats by Dr. Dre line of headphones. The headphones quickly became a cultural phenomenon, and the duo sold the company to Apple for $3 billion in 2014.

After the sale of Beats Electronics, Iovine became an executive at Apple, overseeing Apple Music and working closely with new artists. In 2020, it was announced that Iovine was stepping back from his role at Apple and would be focusing on his health and new projects.

Jimmy Iovine’s Top Songs

While Jimmy Iovine is best known for his work behind the scenes, he has also contributed to some of the most significant hits of the past few decades. Some of his top songs include:

– “Born to Run” by Bruce Springsteen
– “Refugee” by Tom Petty and the Heartbreakers
– “Don’t Stop” by Fleetwood Mac
– “I Will Always Love You” by Whitney Houston
– “California Love” by 2Pac featuring Dr. Dre

Jimmy Iovine’s Net Worth

Jimmy Iovine’s net worth is estimated to be $950 million. He has earned his fortune through his work as a music producer, record executive, and entrepreneur.

Conclusion

Jimmy Iovine’s contributions to the music industry cannot be understated. Throughout his career, he has had a hand in creating some of the most memorable and influential songs of all time. He has also been a pioneer in the business side of music, co-founding Interscope Records and selling Beats Electronics to Apple for billions of dollars. At 68 years old, Jimmy Iovine’s legacy in music and business is sure to endure for decades to come.
