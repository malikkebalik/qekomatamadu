Who is Nate Robinson?

Nate Robinson is a well-known American professional basketball player who has been playing basketball since high school. He is best known as a player from Delaware 87ers wearing jersey number 1.

He is also commonly known as an author who has written ‘Heart Over Height’.

Nate Robinson: Bio, Age, Parents, Siblings, Family, Ethnicity, Nationality

Nate Robinson was born in Seattle, Washington on 31 May 1984. He is of African-American ethnicity and has American nationality.

He is the son of American footballer Jacque Robinson and a beauty salon operator Renee Busch. His birth name is Nathaniel Cornelius Robinson.

He has a sister named Jasmine Stewart and two brothers namely Anthony Stewart and Deron Isaiah Robinson.

Education, School/College University

For his schooling, he joined Rainer Beach High School in Seattle and moved to James Logan High School. He joined Washington on a football scholarship but decided to concentrate on basketball.

Nate Robinson: Professional Life, Career

Nate Robinson started playing basketball professionally as soon as he graduated. He is active in the sports field since 2005 and he is still playing.

Nate started playing with New York Knicks in 1998 when he became the first three-time Slam Dunk Champion.

He left the team in 2010 to join the Boston Celtics. In Celtics, he played just for a year and was traded to the Oklahoma City Thunder in 2011.

He signed a contract with Golden State Warriors on 4 January 2012 where he led the team with 24 points victory over the Miami Heat in the absence of Stephen Curry.

On 31 July 2012, he signed a one-year contract with Chicago Bulls, and on 26 July 2013, he signed a deal with Denver Nuggets.

He was traded once again to Boston Celtics in exchange for Jameer Nelson. On 7 March 2015, he signed 10 days contract with Los Angeles Clippers and later made a year deal with New Orleans Pelicans on 16 October 2015.

Robinson signed with Hapoel Tel Aviv of the Israeli Basketball Premier League on 17 March 2016.

He signed a contract with the Delaware 87ers of the NBA Development League on 8 February 2017 and he is currently playing for the 87ers.

Besides his NBA career, he is also an author who has written ‘Heart Over Height’. He has also opened up a chicken and waffles restaurant in Rainier Beach, Seattle.
