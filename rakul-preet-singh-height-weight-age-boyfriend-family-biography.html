Rakul Preet Singh is an Indian actress, model, and beauty queen who is best known for her work in the Telugu and Tamil show biz. However, the beautiful actress has also starred in many Hindi and Kannada hit films like Aiyaary (2018), Yaariyan (2014), and Gilli (2009). A few of Rakul’s notable Telugu appearances were in Venkatadri Express (2013), Loukyam (2014), Nannaku Prematho (2016), Kick 2 (2015), and Bruce Lee – The Fighter (2015). As a part of her acting career, she has worked with many leading actors from the south like Ravi Teja, Ram Charan, Jr. Ntr, Allu Arjun, as well as Bollywood actors like Ajay Devgn, Tabu, Ritesh Deshmukh, and Sidharth Malhotra. Rakul has also won many beauty pageants like People’s Choice Miss Indiatimes 58888 and the Pantaloons Femina Miss Fresh Face. She has also amassed a huge fan base with more than 8.5 million followers on Instagram, more than 8 million followers on Facebook, and more than 5 million followers on Twitter.

    Rakul Preet Singh Facts

  • She was raised in Delhi. Her father Rajender is an army veteran.
  • Rakul had always known that she wanted to be an actress when she grew up.
  • Her first name, Rakul is a combination of her father’s first two initials from his name and her mother’s first 3 initials from her name.
  • Rakul was in grade 2 when she started taking karate lessons. She went on to attain a blue belt in the combat sport. Later, she started playing many other games like lawn tennis and golf.
  • She was in college when she started modeling at the age of 18.
  • Rakul was 19 years old when she got cast in the film Gilli in 2009.
  • In 2011, she was voted as the ‘People’s Choice Miss Indiatimes 58888’. The same year she also ranked at 5th place during the ‘Femina Miss India’ pageant.
  • In the past, she took up an active franchise of gyms called Functional 45 Training. The gyms can be found in many cities including Gachibowli, Kokapet, and Visakhapatnam.
  • In 2013, Rakul started an eponymous YouTube channel to which she uploads trailers, vlogs, and workout videos.
  • Rakul once stated in an interview with Deccan Chronicle, that she had agreed to star in her debut film Gilli “with the aim to earn a little extra pocket money”.
  • In 2016, Deccan Chronicle reported that Rakul charged a huge remuneration of INR 1.5 crore to act in director Boyapati Srinu’s Jaya Janaki Nayaka, which was released in 2017.
  • In the past, Rakul bought a 3 bedroom house in Lotus Pond, Jubilee Hills, Hyderabad to live with her parents. The apartment cost an astonishing INR 3 crore.
  • Besides being an actor and beauty queen, Rakul is also a national level golfer and a trained equestrian.
  • In 2018, Rakul was featured on the cover of Maxim‘s February edition.
  • She is a skilled Bharatanatyam dancer.
  • In January 2019, Rakul was body-shamed on Twitter. People accused her of sleeping with someone in her car by judging the attire she chose to wear on that day. However, she got back at the trollers with a rather strong yet timid tweet.
  • In December 2020, Rakul was tested positive for coronavirus.
  • In May 2023, Rakul underwent cryotherapy at -15 degree celsius by taking a dip in ice-cold water in swimwear.
