Who is Justin Prentice?

Justin Prentice is a famous American actor and producer famous for his role of Bryce Walker on Selena Gomez‘s production Netflix TV series 13 Reasons Why. He worked alongside Devin Druid, Chelsea Alden, Brandon Larracuente and Katherine Langford as his co-actors in this series. Justin earned fame with his role as Bryce in the comedy tv series Those Who Can’t.

The popular TV star, Justin appeared on various TV series, such as iCarly, Victorious, Melissa and Joey, The Middle, and Criminal Minds. Moreover, he got his first break in 2008 and has appeared in a short video Store Front in the role of a Cookie Boy.
