Rick Ross Loses His Sh-t As Parachute Guy Crashes His Pool Party

Publish date: 2024-07-24

Rick Ross is always good for a viral story or two every time he hosts an event, and his most recent get-together has proven no different.

On Sunday (July 2), the Miami rapper hosted a pool party at his Promise Land estate in Georgia. Aside from a clip of him botching a diving attempt, Rozay is now making the rounds online after he posted footage of a man parachuting into his property during the jamboree.

“Told me the parachute guy landed somewhere. Where he at?” Rozay says in the clip while looking directly at his phone’s camera.

The video then cuts to the person in question descending upon Rick Ross’ property, with him saying: “There he go. Oh my god … oh my god, the parachute guy is goddamn [laughing] … Ay don’t land on my roof!”

Uninvited parachuters seem to have become a recurring theme at the “Hustlin’” rapper’s parties. In early June, he hosted his annual car show at the same location as the pool party.

Soon after the event, a video surfaced online showing Ross addressing a fan who had apparently parachuted into the gathering.

While people might think he would’ve been upset, Da Boss contrarily seemed delighted that someone went above and beyond to attend his event. Smiling in the video, he explained the situation and even asked to see the footage.

As Ross made clear, he admired the parachuting fan and even hoped they wouldn’t be arrested. He did, however, express displeasure at not receiving any notice.

AD LOADING...

“Only thing I don’t like about it is he didn’t tell me what time to expect him jumping out of the goddam plane and landing in my backyard,” he said. “By the time I ran back there, they was dragging his ass … and he had a huge smile on his face and said, ‘Please, please, please don’t press charges.’”

However, Ross joked if someone jumped the gate, he “will do you dirty,” but parachuters just need to give him some notice.

As of now, it is unclear if the parachuter from the car show is the same as the one at the pool party.
