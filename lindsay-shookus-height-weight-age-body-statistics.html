Lindsay Shookus is an American TV producer who is best known for her work as an associate producer, co-producer, and later the producer of the iconic late-night live TV sketch comedy and variety show Saturday Night Live (SNL). For her work on the show, she had been nominated for numerous Emmy Awards. Between 2008 and 2010, she had also been credited as a producer on 45 episodes of the satirical sitcom 30 Rock.

    Lindsay Shookus Facts

  • Lindsay, after she graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in 2002, was hired as TV producer Marci Klein’s assistant on Saturday Night Live. Marci is the daughter of iconic fashion designer Calvin Klein.
  • She became an associate producer on the show in 2008 and was promoted to the post of co-producer in 2010. In 2012, she began to be credited as the show’s producer. She had also worked as the head of the show’s talent department, a role that involved booking hosts and musical guests and scouting potential cast members.
  • In both 2015 and 2016, she was included in the list of the ’50 Most Powerful Music Executives’ that was compiled by the popular American magazine and website Billboard.
  • She and her friend Kristin O’Keeffe Merrick have hosted, for quite some time, an annual networking event in New York titled Women Work F*cking Hard. The objective of the event is to shine a light on the work of female entrepreneurs and encourage women to help each other.
  • In August 2022, Lindsay announced that she was leaving Saturday Night Live after working on the show for almost 20 years.
