Taylor Swift and Harry Styles’ Chemistry Issues

Both singers are attacking the other’s talents for affection. A source close to Harry told Star that he was “telling friends Taylor was asexual and wouldn’t let him do anything. She acted really sexy at first, but once she hooked up with him, she only wanted to kiss.”

Well Harry, maybe it was you, not her. A friend of the Taylor’s told Star that Harry, 18, “kissed like a snail. Eventually Taylor found it hard not to be grossed out.”

No one wants to kiss a snail!

So even though the 23 year-old country singer was reportedly having “trust issues” and “couldn’t cope with all the attention Harry gets from fans, and the fact he is a massive flirt with girls,” the couple’s lack of chemistry” must have been just as big of an issue.

Moving Past the Breakup

While this is probably just a case of bitter exes slinging insults to dull the pain, both stars seem to be acknowledging that you can’t force attraction. Taylor recently traveling to Harry’s hometown, London, but she doesn’t seem to be hanging onto her pop star ex. She’s been having fun with Ed Sheeran and spending time with BFF Selena Gomez, who is going through a breakup of her own with Justin Bieber.

Meanwhile, Harry’s been tweet-flirting with Harry Potter star, Emma Watson. Let’s just hope she doesn’t find his kissing to be as slimy as Taylor did!
