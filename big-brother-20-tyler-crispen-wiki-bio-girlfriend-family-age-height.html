Tyler Crispen Girlfriend and Dating Affair

Tyler Crispen is one of the contestants of the reality shows Big Brother 20 who hasn’t revealed his past relationship before coming into the house. He has been tight-lipped about his personal life and relationship status.

However, the former lifeguard, Tyler was in a relationship with his on-screen girlfriend, Kaitlyn Herman. But after the eviction of Kaitlyn, Tyler was seen exchanging ‘I Love You’s’ with Angela Rummans during an intimate moment on the live feeds on 13th September 2018.

Angela told I’m in love with you to her castmate, Tyler after she revealed she had a secret. Tyler replied during the exchange:

“I can’t believe we have the same secret.”

Angela added:

“I can’t believe that just came out of my mouth.”

The two, who are both from Hilton Head, South Carolina, had a flirty relationship for weeks before they kissed or got physical with one another. Both Angela and Tyler are worried about how getting into a relationship would affect their gameplay on Big Brother. Their chemistry, however, proved to be undeniable.

Kaitlyn Herman, who also had a flirty relationship with Tyler during her time in the house, revealed she “didn’t expect” that the two would pair up during an interview last month.
