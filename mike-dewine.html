Mike DeWine is an attorney and politician from the United States. He is a Republican Party supporter. Since January 14, 2019, he has served as the 70th governor of Ohio. DeWine is a former senator from Ohio. From 2011 to 2019, he was the Attorney General of Ohio.

  • Later, after 18 years in Congress, U.S. Representative Bud Brown of Ohio’s 7th congressional district retired; his father, Clarence Brown, Sr., had previously held the position for 26 years.
  • He was elected in November after winning the Republican nomination.
  • He was re-elected three times in this district, which extends from Springfield to the outskirts of Columbus.
  • In 1986, he ran unopposed in what was widely viewed as a poor year for Republicans across the country. He resigned his position in 1990 to compete for Lieutenant Governor of Ohio as George Voinovich’s running mate.
  • The Voinovich-DeWine ticket was re-elected with ease.
