Nikane Madeira is a rapper and has been making music for a while. He is still in the early stages, and it will take time to start making big money. His net worth is far from some of the biggest rappers. His current net worth is estimated to be around $50 thousand. 

Younger sister Kiana Madeira has already started making a good living as an actress. She has made millions through acting and has an estimated net worth of $1.5 Million. Most of her net worth comes from theater, thanks to the success of films like Bad Hair Day, The Swap, and Fear Street.

Kiana is the only one in the Madeira family with acting skill. Nikane's other sister Kyla Madeira has also decided to make a career in the film industry.

About His Sister Kiana Madeira

Kiana Madeira is an actress mostly known for her television works. The Canadian beauty hailed from Toronto and was born on November 4, 1992. She might be short in height, with the woman standing at 5 Feet and 5 Inches (165 cm). 

However, that didn't stop her from pursuing her dream of becoming an actor. She has wanted to become an actor since she watched Grease as a kid.

Her IMDb page says she started giving auditions when she was just 10. Fast forward to the present day, and she is an established television actress, having worked in popular shows like The Flash (The show has a vast cast, including actress Candice Patton). Her other works are Fear Street, After Ever Happy, Level 16, and She Never Died.

He Has A Mixed Ethnicity (About His Parents)

Nikane Madeira is from Canada, but his ethnicity is vast. His ancestors go back to a lot of different places. So, what is Nikane made up of? He is Half Portuguese, Half First Nations, Half Irish, and Half Black. He gets his Portuguese from his father, whereas the First Nations, Irish, and Black are from his mother. 

There isn't much detail available on his parents. So, their names, profession, and whereabouts remain unknown. But, Kiana, in an interview with L'Officiel, has talked about what it was like growing up with her parents, which gives us a bit of an idea of how Nikane's parents are. Upon asked why she chose to become an actor, she said:

I come from a family of storytellers; I’ve grown up in a household where we are constantly cracking jokes, sharing stories, and brainstorming ways to create. Being surrounded by humans who are not afraid to feel deeply and outwardly express those feelings had a huge influence on my desire to pursue a career as an actor. I have always admired truthful, bold expression of emotion and since I was a child I have been very curious to learn more about how and why we feel things as people.
