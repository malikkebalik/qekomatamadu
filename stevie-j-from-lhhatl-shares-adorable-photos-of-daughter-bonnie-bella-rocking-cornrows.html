Stevie J from LHHATL Shares Adorable Photos of Daughter Bonnie Bella Rocking Cornrows

Publish date: 2024-07-20

Stevie J gushed about his daughter, Bonnie, on Instagram as he showed off how adorable his little girl looks in braids, and his followers reacted.

Stevie J is a proud father who loves to show off his children. This time, the spotlight is on his little girl, Bonnie, and he showed her off to his 2.3 million Instagram followers.

The famous DJ shared a photo slide of the young girl rocking braids. The hairdo started with cornrows at the base, and ended with a twist at the tip, making the little girl look adorable.

Stevie J at the 2019 Finding Ashley Stewart Finale Event on September 14, 2019 | Photo: Getty Images

THE DOLL CALLED BONNIE

The snaps captured both the front, side, and back of the braids, and little Bonnie dressed in a printed sweater smiled for the camera. Her father captioned it:

"My doll Bonnie J. #DaJordans. Thanks, @amber4hair."

The sweet post got a lot of positive reactions, with the little girl's mom, Joseline Hernandez, leaving an "Omg" comment. One follower of the DJ left heart-shaped emojis, and the word, "The cutest."

A BEAUTIFUL PRINCESS

Another person agreed with the proud dad writing that his daughter is a doll and a princess, and her hair looks absolutely beautiful. One follower wrote, "Go Bonnie" adding that the joy on the little girl's face is evident.

Stevie J appears to have been spending time with his daughter. He shares custody of the little girl with Hernandez, whom he was engaged to.

A MARVELOUS SOUL

The former couple welcomed the little girl in 2016 and celebrated her third birthday in December. Taking to Instagram at the time, the music producer wrote:

"12.28 marks the day a marvelous soul was born. Bonnie Bella Jordan, thank you for brightening most of my days and nights, I love you endlessly."

He loves being able to make moments with his little girl and being an active part of her life. Stevie J went on to thank Hernandez for giving him Bonnie, his priceless baby doll.

ABOVE PERSONAL DIFFERENCES

The dad-of-six — he has five children from past relationships — and his little girl shares a strong resemblance. It makes their bond stronger, and it is a testament that the DJ has active genes.

Stevie J and Hernandez have a cordial, co-parenting relationship. They put their daughter above their differences and do what's best for her always.
