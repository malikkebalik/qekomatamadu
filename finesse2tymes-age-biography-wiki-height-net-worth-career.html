Finesse2tymes is an American rapper who is known for his singles “First 48”, “Back End” and “Gucci Flow”. In 2019, he released his first mixtape titled Hustle & Flow. Finesse2tymes Biography, Age

Finesse2tymes’s age is 30 years old, as in 2023. He was born on June 10, 1992 in Memphis, TN. His zodiac sign is, Gemini. His birth name is Ricky Hampton. When he was 21, Hampton, along with Moneybagg Yo, Blac Youngsta, and other Memphis rappers, formed the rap collective Memphis Greatest Underrated, in 2012. The group released a self-titled EP in 2016.
