Idaho Lottery director guarantees two $1 million tickets this year in Idaho raffle game

Publish date: 2024-07-22

BOISE, Idaho (CBS2) — The director of the Idaho Lottery personally guarantees that two separate $1 million winning tickets will be sold in Idaho this year.

The Idaho $1,000,000 Raffle is launching again for the 17th edition. Last year, the Idaho Lottery reported the raffle game sold out in just 22 days, ending early on November 12, 2022.

This year the Idaho Lottery has decided to change things up a bit for this year, increasing the number of tickets to 450,000, but promising two one-million-dollar prizes. Tickets for the Idaho $1,000,000 Raffle go on sale Saturday, October 21, for $10 a piece.

“I personally guarantee the odds to win one million dollars is better than ever and the Idaho Lottery will sell two winning tickets worth $1,000,000 each!” declared Idaho Lottery Director Jeff Anderson.

The raffle tickets will remain on sale until December 26, 2023, or until the tickets sell out.
